Macro / Policy / Regime Deep Read

Regime summary: market conditions remain "selective risk-on with frequent stress pockets." This is the type of tape where quality balance sheets and earnings visibility continue to attract capital, while crowded or narrative-heavy names can still suffer sharp drawdowns on even minor disappointments.

Rates and valuation transmission: equity multiples are still rate-sensitive. If real yields re-accelerate, long-duration growth names can reprice quickly even without an earnings miss. That argues for confirmation-based entries: buy strength that proves itself, not hypothetical value based on prior highs.

Dollar and global demand linkage: a firmer USD can tighten global financial conditions and pressure non-US demand translation, especially for semis/hardware exporters. For this portfolio, keep an eye on semis + industrial tech when DXY rises alongside yields.

Energy/inflation channel: oil volatility keeps inflation expectations "sticky enough" to challenge fast policy easing narratives. If oil and wages stay firm, Fed easing could remain shallow, limiting upside from pure multiple expansion.

Positioning implication: run a barbell: (1) core cash-flow durable leaders (mega-cap platform + mission-critical infra) and (2) smaller tactical sleeve for defense/space/high-beta AI. Use smaller initial size in the tactical sleeve and only pyramid when price confirms above trigger with volume.

Risk protocol: treat invalidation levels as hard rules. In this regime, failed breakouts often mean swift downside air pockets. Reduce exposure quickly when negation criteria are met rather than waiting for narratives to improve.

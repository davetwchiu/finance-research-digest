Policy / Macro Catalyst Map

Policy is still dominated by the Fed inflation-vs-growth balancing act, US fiscal/defense spending persistence, and semiconductor export-control implementation risk. Portfolio implication: keep a core in cash-flow durable leaders, and treat high-beta tactical entries as confirmation-dependent trades rather than valuation-only buys.

Scenario map (1-3 months):

  • Base (45%): soft-landing with uneven disinflation. Quality compounders and infra beneficiaries lead; tactical small-cap breakouts still work but fail faster.
  • Sticky inflation (35%): cut expectations push right; duration compresses; valuation-sensitive growth underperforms unless earnings beats are clear.
  • Growth wobble (20%): yields compress, defensives and mega-cap quality outperform; speculative beta rallies become tradable bursts not persistent trends.

  • For high-beta names, require close-based confirmation above trigger before adding full size.
  • Use staged exits: trim 30-50% at T1, let remainder run to T2 only if volume supports.
  • If trigger fails same/next session and closes back inside range, treat as negation and de-risk quickly.

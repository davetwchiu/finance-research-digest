## Headline
Cantor Fitzgerald Upgrades Tesla (TSLA) to Overweight, $425 Price Target

## Body
Cantor Fitzgerald analyst Andres Sheppard upgraded Tesla from Neutral to Overweight, citing attractive entry point ahead of material catalysts including 2026 Cybercab launch and ongoing Robotaxi rollout in Texas/California. Analyst also highlighted Optimus humanoid robot program as long-term value driver.

## Why It Matters
The upgrade counters recent EV sector weakness amid elevated oil prices and geopolitical tensions. Tesla remains the top bet in the AI/robotics narrative, with Cybercab being the primary catalyst investors are waiting for. Optimus potential could ultimately outpace automotive business valuation.

## Watchlist Impact
- **Direct Impact:** TSLA – Immediate catalyst from analyst rating change, price target at $425
- **Second-Order:** Positive sentiment across EV sector as rate cut expectations shift; Neutral impact on NVDA as GTC conference begins today

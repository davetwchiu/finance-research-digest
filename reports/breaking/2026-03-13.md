### Palantir and Ondas just formalized a filing-backed ISR partnership with World View

Ondas disclosed in an 8-K that it, Palantir, and World View entered a strategic partnership to build a multi-domain ISR stack linking stratospheric balloons, autonomous aerial and ground systems, and Palantir’s AIP software. The filing says work is already underway and integration across Ondas’ portfolio could begin as early as 4Q26, but it does not disclose contract economics.

**Why it matters**
This is more than another AI-defense headline because it gives **ONDS** a clearer path into a broader persistent-ISR architecture and gives **PLTR** another concrete defense workflow anchored in official company disclosure. The read should still stay tight: this is partnership validation and platform positioning, not booked revenue.

**Watchlist impact**
Direct: **ONDS, PLTR**.
Second-order: mildly supportive for **KTOS, RDW, RKLB, BBAI** if investors lean further into integrated defense-autonomy platforms, but the immediate catalyst is still specific to Ondas and Palantir.

### Palantir and NVIDIA just teamed up on a sovereign AI reference stack

Palantir announced a sovereign AI operating-system reference architecture with NVIDIA aimed at customers that need on-premise, edge, or sovereign-cloud control over models, data, and deployment. The stack pairs Palantir’s AIP/Foundry/Apollo/Rubix layer with NVIDIA Blackwell Ultra systems and networking, positioning the offer as a turnkey AI datacenter blueprint rather than a point integration.

**Why it matters**
This pushes **PLTR** further up the stack from application software toward full deployment architecture, while giving **NVDA** another enterprise/government path to package accelerated compute with a named software control layer. The key limitation: this is architecture and go-to-market positioning, not a disclosed customer win or revenue number.

**Watchlist impact**
Direct: **PLTR, NVDA**.
Second-order: mildly supportive for **AVGO** if sovereign build-outs keep favoring full-stack AI infrastructure, but the immediate read is concentrated in Palantir’s enterprise/government positioning and NVIDIA demand capture.

## 08:53 HKT / 2026-03-13 00:53 UTC

### Palantir and Ondas just formalized a filing-backed ISR partnership with World View

Ondas disclosed in an 8-K that it, Palantir, and World View entered a strategic partnership to build a multi-domain ISR stack linking stratospheric balloons, autonomous aerial and ground systems, and Palantir’s AIP software. The filing says work is already underway and integration across Ondas’ portfolio could begin as early as 4Q26, but it does not disclose contract economics.

**Why it matters**
This is more than another AI-defense headline because it gives **ONDS** a clearer path into a broader persistent-ISR architecture and gives **PLTR** another concrete defense workflow anchored in official company disclosure. The read should still stay tight: this is partnership validation and platform positioning, not booked revenue.

**Watchlist impact**
Direct: **ONDS, PLTR**.
Second-order: mildly supportive for **KTOS, RDW, RKLB, BBAI** if investors lean further into integrated defense-autonomy platforms, but the immediate catalyst is still specific to Ondas and Palantir.

Source note: based on Ondas' March 12 8-K and attached press materials; no contract value or customer award was disclosed, so the interpretation stays narrow.

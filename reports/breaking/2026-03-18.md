## Lululemon Misses Guidance on Weak 2026 Outlook

**Body:** Lululemon (LULU) lowered fiscal 2026 guidance on Tuesday, with Q1 2026 sales guidance of $2.40–$2.43B (vs. $2.47B estimated) and EPS guidance of $1.63–$1.68 (vs. $2.07 estimated). Full-year sales guidance of $11.35–$11.50B also came in below the $11.52B market expectation.

**Why it matters:** Tariffs ($380M gross, up $105M vs. 2025, $220M net) and the proxy battle with founder Chip Wilson are pressuring margins. The company explicitly stated it will not pass tariff costs to consumers, and same-store sales in the Americas are flat after two years of growth, with 1–3% sales declines expected in 2026.

**Watchlist impact:** **LULU — direct earnings miss, lower near-term sentiment.** Second-order: consumer discretionary goods sector headwinds from prolonged tariff rhetoric.

# USTR says U.S. tariff rate will rise to 15% or higher for some countries

- **Time (HKT):** 2026-02-25 21:57 HKT
- **Ticker(s):** Macro (watchlist sensitivity: AAPL, AVGO, MSFT, NVDA, GOOG, TSLA, TSM, IBM, BRK.B, LITE)
- **Type:** Macro policy shock (trade/tariff escalation)
- **Why material:** This is a stronger official escalation signal than prior “working toward 15%” guidance and implies higher trade-policy pressure for exposed sectors.

## What happened
Reuters reported USTR Jamieson Greer said tariff rates for some countries will move from 10% to 15% or higher, without naming specific countries. The update increases probability of broader tariff pass-through and policy-volatility persistence.

## Potential positioning impact
- Keeps macro risk premium elevated across globally exposed equities and semis.
- Raises margin/valuation uncertainty for import-dependent and supply-chain-sensitive names.
- Increases downside scenario weight into upcoming U.S. macro/earnings catalysts.
